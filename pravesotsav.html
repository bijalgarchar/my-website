About School Pravesotsav

Pravesotsav is a special event marking the beginning of a new academic year, celebrated with great enthusiasm at Dhudkot Taluka School. It is a day of welcoming new students, fostering a love for learning, and setting the tone for an exciting academic journey ahead.

During this event, students, parents, and teachers come together to celebrate the start of the school year with speeches, cultural performances, and games. The school ensures a warm welcome for every child entering the school for the first time.

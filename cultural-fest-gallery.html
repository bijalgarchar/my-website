About Our Bal-melo

The Dhudkot Taluka School Bal-melo is an annual event celebrating the rich diversity and talent of our students. It is a vibrant showcase of traditional dances, musical performances, art exhibitions, and drama productions, all aimed at promoting cultural understanding and creative expression. Students from different grades participate in this exciting event, making it a highlight of our school year.

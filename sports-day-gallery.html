Event Highlights

The Annual Sports Day was held on February 15, 2024. The day was filled with thrilling competitions, cheering crowds, and inspiring displays of teamwork. Students from all grades participated in various events such as Chess, Street Game, Flying Kite, and more. The spirit of sportsmanship was alive as each house competed for the championship!

Key Events: 100m Race, Long Jump, Chess, Sack Race, Relay Race, and more.

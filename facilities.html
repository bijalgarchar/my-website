Digital Smartboard

A smart board, also known as an interactive whiteboard, is a large touch-sensitive display that combines a traditional whiteboard with digital technology. Teachers use smart boards to create engaging lessons and promote active student participation.

Mid Day Meal

The Mid-Day Meal (MDM) scheme is a government program that provides free, nutritious meals to children in government and government-aided schools in India. The scheme aims to improve the nutritional status of children and increase school attendance and retention rates.

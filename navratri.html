About Navaratri Celebration

Navaratri is a joyous and colorful festival celebrated across India, and at Dhudkot Taluka School, it is a time of great excitement and unity. The nine-day celebration is filled with devotion, music, and traditional Garba and Dandiya performances by students and staff alike. It's an occasion to honor the victory of good over evil and to celebrate the strength and grace of the Goddess Durga.
